Dear Mr. Somebody: Asexual Romantic Comedy

Think dating in the new age is hard? Well, try being a 24 year old, borderline asexual woman that is completely against the idea of having kids or getting married. Somehow, Alfie still holds onto the hope of finding the one. Luckily, fate is on her side and her life takes an unexpected turn when she meets Fin, a 26 year old weirdo, that’s still trying to get his life together.

Between the childish shenanigans, hilarious misunderstandings, morbid jokes, borderline psycho dates, a very involved and super religious mother, and their two wacky friends, they learn that relationships aren’t easy, but love is worth it.
