About Keyla Jones

Keyla Jones is a budding writer with two complete manuscripts and four works-in-progress. She also wrote and directed an award winning play in Glendale, Arizona.

Aside from her writing, Keyla is also a performing musician and artist. She has performed at many music festivals around the U.S.A.
